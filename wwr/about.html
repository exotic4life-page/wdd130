White Water Rafting Co.

At White Water Rafting Co., we are passionate about providing thrilling and unforgettable rafting experiences. Our team of experienced guides is dedicated to ensuring your safety and enjoyment on the water, from your first paddle stroke to your last splash.

History

Founded over two decades ago by a small group of river guides, White Water Rafting Co. began with a single raft and a big dream: to share the beauty and thrill of the river with everyone who was brave enough to hop in. Since then, we've grown into one of the region's most trusted outfitters, guiding thousands of adventurers safely down rapids of every class, while staying true to our founding values of safety, stewardship of the river, and unforgettable fun.
