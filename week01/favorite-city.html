Lagos, Nigeria

Lagos is my favorite city. It is the largest city in Nigeria and one of the fastest-growing urban areas in Africa. Known for its vibrant energy, bustling markets, and beautiful coastline along the Atlantic Ocean, Lagos is a place where tradition and modern city life come together.

What I love most about Lagos is the sense of hustle and opportunity you feel everywhere you go. From the busy streets of Ikeja to the scenic views of Lekki, the city is full of culture, delicious food, and friendly people who make it feel like home.
